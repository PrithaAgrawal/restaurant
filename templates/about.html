About Us

Welcome to Lemons, where the vibrant flavors of Italy come to life! Nestled in the heart of Delhi, our restaurant is a celebration of authentic Italian cuisine, crafted with passion and the freshest ingredients. From perfectly brewed espressos to handmade desserts, we invite you to explore Italy’s culinary traditions, with dishes inspired by Tuscany, Naples, and the Amalfi Coast. Whether it’s our zesty Spaghetti al Limone or in-house Limoncello, every dish promises love, precision, and excellence. Join us at Lemons, a slice of Italy in India! Buon appetito!

At Lemon’s Italian Restaurant, our story is deeply rooted in family, tradition, and a passion for authentic flavors. Inspired by our beloved Nonna, who spent countless hours in her cozy kitchen crafting recipes passed down through generations, we wanted to create a place where everyone could experience the warmth and love that comes with a home-cooked Italian meal. Nonna’s secret tomato sauce, her perfectly hand-rolled gnocchi, and her insistence on "cooking with the heart" became the cornerstone of our vision. Lemon’s is not just a restaurant—it’s a tribute to her legacy and a celebration of the Italian way of bringing people together over delicious food and unforgettable memories.
